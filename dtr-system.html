Standalone DTR System

Overview

The Standalone DTR System was developed as a supplementary attendance management solution designed to support an existing web-based DTR system.

Although the organization already had an online attendance platform, there were instances where attendance records appeared blank or were not properly recorded despite successful employee logins.

Problem It Solves

Because attendance records occasionally failed to appear in the online system, employees often maintained manual attendance logs as a backup reference.

To reduce reliance on manual record keeping, I developed a standalone DTR application capable of generating attendance reports and providing an additional layer of attendance verification.

Graveyard Shift Logic

One of the major challenges was supporting overnight schedules.

The system correctly handles shifts that cross midnight by recognizing that the employee's Time In and Time Out belong to the same work schedule.

  • Time In: 10:00 PM
  • Time Out: 7:00 AM (Next Day)

Instead of splitting the attendance into two separate dates, the system calculates the total working hours as a single shift.

Features

  • Backup attendance recording
  • Attendance verification
  • Date range reporting
  • Graveyard shift support
  • Employee attendance history
  • Export to Microsoft Excel
  • Export to Microsoft Word
  • Printable attendance reports
